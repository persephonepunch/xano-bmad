## Quick FAQ

**Q: How is this different from social media?**
A: Long-form storytelling (not 280 chars), university-specific privacy controls, no algorithm manipulation, combined with financial support system.

**Q: How is this different from WordPress/Medium?**
A: Fine-grained access control (permit.io ABAC), integrated endowments, student-editable, cost-optimized (<$200/month).

**Q: Why would corporations participate?**
A: Direct marketing to 500+ engaged students (opt-in only), brand alignment with student success, measurable ROI, ethical advertising model.

**Q: How do students benefit from corporate partnerships?**
A: Unlock PREMIUM features (privacy, groups) + receive discount codes/loyalty offers. It's a value exchange, not forced advertising.

**Q: Is student data safe?**
A: Yes. FERPA compliant, explicit opt-in required for corporate data sharing, students control their privacy, audit logging for all actions.
